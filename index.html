Faddeev Popov formalism

Gauge field theory is a hot topic in modern quantum field theory, and Faddeev and Popov are the first ones who tried to apply path integral to gauge field theory instead of canonical quantization as Dirac did. The consequence of quantizing gauge field is the introduction of ghost fields, which are necessarily a quantum (loop) effect.

Faddeev Popov formalism directly leads to the introduction of BRST formalism, which is more general than the former one.
