Brief Introduction to Effective Theory of Binary Inspirals

Ting-kai Hsu
2025-07-12
course_projects
EFT, report

My EFT course report is a brief introduction to the effective theory of binary inspirals.
The attached file is the manuscript of my blackboard presentation.
First, I introduced the setup, including the hierarchy, the power counting parameter, and the action.
I categorized the modes of the gravitational field by their momenta, which is crucial for accurately calculating the effective action, including both the potential and the radiation.
Then I calculate the effective potential term between the binary inspirals, and I emphasize the use of Feynman diagrams and the power counting, which enable us to reproduce the post-Newtonian potential terms term by term in this EFT formalism.
